Butter Paneer

Description

Learn how to make restaurant style Paneer Butter Masala at home, with step-by-step images whether you are a seasoned cook or a beginner. It is rich, creamy tomato based gravy with succulent Indian cottage cheese and also never fail to impress.

Paneer Butter Masala also known as simply butter paneer is a popular Indian recipe. Widely known throughout the world, that is most of the Indian restaurants have this as main side dish in their menu card. Buttery, creamy as well as rich side dish for flatbread with Indian cottage cheese Paneer is such a delight for taste buds. Besides, it is very easy to prepare this at home if you follow the steps.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Tomato
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Spices
  • Green Chilli
  • Milk

Steps

  1. To begin with, heat a pan with oil, 1 teaspoon butter. After that, add garlic, ginger and green chillies.Then, tip in cubed onion and give it a fry.
  2. Add cashews and saute until onion turn translucent.Now, place the cooled items in a blender.After that, add cubed tomatoes (yes, raw!) on top of it.
  3. Then, grind to a smooth puree.After that, heat oil and temper with Cardamom, cinnamon and cloves.
  4. Add the ground onion tomato masala and tip in red chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric, kasoori methi and saute well.Continue frying the masala for 4-5 minutes in medium heat or until the raw smell goes off.
  5. The onion tomato masala should be thick, shiny and ooze oil. Add oil in between if needed.After that, pour milk little at a time in low heat and stir well. Repeat again until you add all the milk to masala.
  6. Adjust water (I added Â¼ cup) and sprinkle required salt. Once it start to bubble again, add paneer drained from water.
  7. Mix well and in goes the cooking cream.Keep the heat in low and don't let the gravy boil vigorously. Just cook in low heat for 2 minutes and switch off the stove.
  8. Add 2 tps of butter and serve with raw corinder sprinkled on top

Voila your Butter Paneeris Ready! Enjoy it with Naan or Rice or simple roti.
